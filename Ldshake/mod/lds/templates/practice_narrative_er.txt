<h1>How to use this rubric</h1>

<p>A rubric is a scaffold for evaluation, a set of guidelines and criteria reflecting an accepted standard of quality. To use this rubric, copy the headings to a comment on the practice narrative and for each one, note your rating (<strong><span style="color:#FF0000;">red</span></strong> = requires major revision, <span style="color:#FF8C00;"><strong>amber</strong></span> = acceptable, <strong><span style="color:#00FF00;">green</span></strong> = high quality) and add a note justifying your rating and providing advice as to how to improve the aspect under inspection.</p>
