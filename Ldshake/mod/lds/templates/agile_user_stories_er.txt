<h1>How to use this rubric</h1>

<p>A rubric is a scaffold for evaluation, a set of guidelines and criteria reflecting an accepted standard of quality. To use this rubric, copy the headings to a comment on the practice narrative and for each one, note your rating (<strong><span style="color: rgb(255, 0, 0);">red</span></strong>&nbsp;= requires major revision,<span style="color: rgb(255, 140, 0);"><strong>amber</strong></span>&nbsp;= acceptable,&nbsp;<strong><span style="color: rgb(0, 255, 0);">green</span></strong>&nbsp;= high quality) and add a note justifying your rating and providing advice as to how to improve the aspect under inspection.</p>
