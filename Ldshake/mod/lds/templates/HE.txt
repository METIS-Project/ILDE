<h1><b><font size=5 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB style='font-size:
18.0pt;line-height:115%'><span style='line-height:115%'>Heuristic Evaluation of
e-Learning: <span style='background:yellow'>&lt;LdS&gt;</span></span></span></font></b></h1>

<p class=Normal1><i><font size=2 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB
style='font-size:11.0pt;line-height:115%;font-style:italic'>See the support
document for instructions for using this template.</span></font></i></p>

<p class=Normal1><font size=2 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB
style='font-size:11.0pt;line-height:115%'>&nbsp;</span></font></p>

<p class=Normal1><font size=2 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB
style='font-size:11.0pt;line-height:115%'>Heuristic evaluation is a technique
borrowed from usability research, where a group of experts is asked to assess a
particular design using a given rubric (set of heuristics). It offers a
low-cost rapid evaluation which often uncovers design flaws at an early stage.</span></font></p>

<p class=Normal1><font size=2 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB
style='font-size:11.0pt;line-height:115%'>Thank you for agreeing to help us
evaluate <span style='background:yellow'>&lt;project name&gt;</span>. Today you
will be asked to put yourself in the learnerâ€™s seat, review the project, and
note any design flaws you perceive. </span></font></p>

<p class=Normal1><font size=2 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB
style='font-size:11.0pt;line-height:115%'>&nbsp;</span></font></p>

<h2><b><font size=4 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB style='font-size:
14.0pt;line-height:115%'><span style='line-height:115%'>Introducing <span
style='background:yellow'>&lt;LdS&gt;</span></span></span></font></b></h2>

<p class=Normal1><i><font size=2 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB
style='font-size:11.0pt;line-height:115%;font-style:italic'>Add a paragraph or
two introducing your LdS: what are you creating, who is the target audience,
what do you aim to achieve?</span></font></i></p>

<h2><b><font size=4 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB style='font-size:
14.0pt;line-height:115%'><span style='line-height:115%'>Your task</span></span></font></b></h2>

<p class=Normal1><i><font size=2 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB
style='font-size:11.0pt;line-height:115%;font-style:italic'>Instruct the
evaluator how to perform the evaluation. Tell them what role they assume, and
what actions they should take. Often, the instructions suggest that the
evaluator first does a quick pass through the evaluated system, then goes
through it systematically and monitors the heuristics (below). You might want
to suggest that evaluators take a quick note of the design flaws they notice,
then return to their score sheet at the end and fill in the gaps. </span></font></i></p>

<p class=Normal1><font size=2 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB
style='font-size:11.0pt;line-height:115%'>&nbsp;</span></font></p>

<h2><b><font size=4 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB style='font-size:
14.0pt;line-height:115%'><span style='line-height:115%'>The Heuristics</span></span></font></b></h2>

<p class=Normal1><i><font size=2 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB
style='font-size:11.0pt;line-height:115%;font-style:italic'>Present the
evaluator with a set of heuristics to apply as they review your LdS. </span></font></i></p>

<p class=Normal1><i><font size=2 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB
style='font-size:11.0pt;line-height:115%;font-style:italic'>Review the
heuristics sets in the appendix. Select the ones that are relevant to your LdS,
edit them to better fit your context, and add your own.</span></font></i></p>

<p class=Normal1><font size=2 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB
style='font-size:11.0pt;line-height:115%'>&nbsp;</span></font></p>

<h2><b><font size=4 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB style='font-size:
14.0pt;line-height:115%'><span style='line-height:115%'>Scoring sheet</span></span></font></b></h2>

<p class=Normal1><i><font size=2 color=black face=Arial><span lang=EN-GB
style='font-size:11.0pt;line-height:115%;font-style:italic'>Provide evaluators
with a scoring sheet to use during the evaluation. </span></font></i></p>
