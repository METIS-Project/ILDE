<blockquote>
<p>In software development and product management, a user story is one or more sentences in the everyday or business language of the end user or user of a system that captures what a user does or needs to do as part of his or her job function. User stories are used with agile software development methodologies as the basis for defining the functions a business system must provide, and to facilitate requirements management. It captures the &#39;who&#39;, &#39;what&#39; and &#39;why&#39; of a requirement in a simple, concise way, often limited in detail by what can be hand-written on a small paper notecard.</p>
</blockquote>
