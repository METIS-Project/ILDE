Anonymous assessment
Problem:

Students might feel uncomfortable when they ar asked to assess their partners. How can we improve the situation in order to encourage students to engage in the assessment process?

Solution:

One possible solution to the students' reticences towards assessing their partners is to guarantee the assessor's anonymity. In this way, the students can feel more comfortable about expressing freely their ideas and opinions about othes' work.

When the students take the role of assessors, an environment that guarantees anonymity enables more fair and precise assessments.

Application guidelines:

In order to complete the application of this pattern, it is recommended:

  • This pattern does not define the assessment technique used, only an additional aspect of the configuration of the assessment. Therefore, it can be used with other patterns that define the assessment technique.
  • In the assess task, the assessor must know that anonymity is guaranteed. This can affect the way the assess task is condicted. This information can be included in the description of the activity.
  • If the assess task is completed through computers, a mechanisms to guarantee anonymity has to be employed.
