Guidelines for application and enactment of this pattern

  • During particularization, several tasks should be done:
    • Choose the activity in which the solution is produced (this activity is not part of the Peer review).
    • Determine the type of review.
    • Complete the activities for teachers and students.
  • During instantiation, the following tasks are necessary:
    • Create the needed groups.
    • Link authors and reviewers.
    • Ensure that the reviewers will have access to the documents they have to review.
